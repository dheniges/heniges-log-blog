On June 10th we continued with our theme of meeting up with our sisters around the world. We flew from Australia to Bali, Indonesia to meet up with my sister and Nick, somehow convincing them it was a good idea to book 24+ hours of travel time in order to meet us there. For sister bonus points we even added a final 1 hour flight from Bali to a nearby neighboring island called Lombok. It's unclear if Nick truly knew what he was getting into flight wise -- sorry Nick!

The next day my sister convinced me, her gullible sidekick, to join her for day 2 of surfing in which we were to go out to the larger wave break, having "graduated" from surfing near the shore. I not only failed epically at the surfing part, but also got humbled by becoming so tired trying to swim the surfboard back to the start line that numerous onlookers inquired as to my well being. You might be surprised to learn I didn't sign up for day 3.

Yes, that happened. They might have questioned Dirk and my outfit choices, but when you're travelling around the world with a carry-on, this is what you get.

I tell you, it was a cultural experience of a different sort.
